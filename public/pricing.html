Access & pricing

Commercial use of the free tier

Individuals, activists, journalists, researchers, and NGOs: use the free tier without restriction beyond attribution. Companies incorporating this data into commercial products or services need an Organization or Enterprise license.

How billing works

  • Quarterly invoicing, payable by USD wire transfer (SWIFT) — standard institutional procurement. Card payment and crypto invoicing available on request.
  • Founding organizations (first three) lock this pricing for 12 months.
  • Field NGOs and humanitarian organizations: we do not charge you. Write to us — researcher-tier access applies.
